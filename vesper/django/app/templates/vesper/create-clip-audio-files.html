Create clip audio files

Creates audio files for the specified clips of this Vesper archive.

{% include "vesper/clips-specification-message.html" %}

The audio files for the recordings of which the clips are parts must be available to supply the clips' audio data. After the clip audio files are created, whenever Vesper needs the clips' audio data it will read them from those files instead of from the recordings.
