Delete Recordings

Deletes recordings from this archive. The recordings are specified as a set of stations and a range of dates. All data associated with the recordings, except for the recording sound files if present, are deleted from the archive, including recording metadata, clips (including clip sound files), and annotations. The deletion runs as a Vesper job. This operation cannot be undone, so be careful!
